About Us 

Technical Guru - Eben Bruyns
Founder/Director

Eben has been developing software for 20+ years and possesses a broad and extremely deep knowledge across most areas of IT. He has always been strongly interested in cyber security and very actively involved in this space since 2008; he leads a tech team of young fellow IT gurus. They are unique in having both programming and security credentials which means we bring real-life relevant results, not just an "it's broken" here and here.

He has a very strong architectural perspective on database and application design, and brings a long-term strategic perspective to design systems which are inherently flexible and able to move with new technologies and business requirements.

Eben was a key part of the team that successfully launched the Konnect Net Platform in New Zealand.

Business Development - Trevor (TJ) Boone
Founder/Director

Trevor has a strong background in IT sales, marketing, project direction and product development gained from very senior roles with IH, Cray Coms and Unisys where he was Account Director for MSD and IRD. He has particular interest and expertise in information security and abit passionate about UX design.

He is also a principal of Theseus Management Group and has worked extensively throughout Australasia on management consulting and IT project implementations particularly in health, insurance, distribution and employment sectors.

Trevor was also a founding shareholder of and consulted to the very successful Konnect Net Ltd, specialising in the SureMed product development and deployment and pivotal in ensuring widespread adoption by GP Practices.
